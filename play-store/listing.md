```
Practice spotting scams safely — before they cost you money, time, or peace of mind.

Most people only learn what a real scam looks like AFTER they've been fooled. Scam Gym flips that. You practice on realistic scam messages — phishing emails, fake invoices, suspicious texts, "your account is locked" alerts — in a safe environment where the wrong answer just costs you a point, not your savings.

WHAT YOU GET
• 700+ realistic drill scenarios across SMS, email, DMs, and more
• Real-world patterns: package delivery scams, IRS and government impersonation, romance scams, job offer scams, AI-generated voice and text fakes, and 20+ more categories
• Confidence tracking — discover when you're guessing vs. genuinely sure (the gap is where scammers get you)
• Personal risk profile showing which scam types you're most vulnerable to
• Weak-spot training that auto-targets your blind spots
• Daily streak tracking and milestone medals

NO ACCOUNT NEEDED
Most of the app works without signing in. Your practice history, stats, and bookmarks stay on your device — we never see them.

OPTIONAL PRO ($9.99 one-time)
Unlock advanced features: accuracy trend charts, per-category deep dives, custom focus, full attempt history, curated 10-drill sessions, bookmarks, daily streaks, and a light theme. One-time payment, no subscription.

WHO IT'S FOR
Anyone who uses a phone or email — but especially valuable for older family members, anyone new to navigating online life, or anyone who's been targeted by a scam before. The drills use plain, friendly language — no tech jargon required.

WHY PRACTICE WORKS
Reading a "watch out for scams" article doesn't change behavior. Repeatedly seeing the patterns and making decisions does. Five minutes a day builds real instincts.

Stay safer. Practice first.
```
